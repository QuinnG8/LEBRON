Welcome to LEBRON - The NBA Player Guessing Game!

Test your knowledge of the NBA with our fun and challenging guessing game! In this game, you will be given a series of hints about a mystery NBA player. Your goal is to guess the correct player based on these clues.

After each guess, you'll receive hints indicating whether the guessed player is in the same conference, has a similar jersey number, is of a comparable age, and more. Use these hints to narrow down your guesses and find the mystery player. It's a great way to see how well you know the NBA and its players!
